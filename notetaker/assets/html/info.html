Help

Action Steps
Create a note Click/Tap on the plus icon in navigation bar or sidebar to create a new note.
Delete a note Hover/Tap on your note title in sidebar you wish to delete, then click/tap on the trash can to delete.
Rename a note Change your note in the editor's title.
Edit title Click/Tap on the title to edit.
Edit content Click/Tap on the content to edit.
Use tools Select the contents you want to use if needed, then click on the tool icon to activate it.
Download .notetaker Open sidebar, click on the share notes button at the left of new icon in sidebar. Click 'Download All Notes'
Upload .notetaker Open sidebar, click on the share notes button at the left of new icon in sidebar. Click the upload box to select a file, or drop a .notetaker file to the box.
